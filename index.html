Our Story

Rooted in Mathura,
Made with Devotion

Kushpreet was born in Manik Chowk, in the heart of Mathura — a city where flavour and faith have mingled for centuries. What began as a small trading house, M/s Kushagra Traders, grew from one simple belief: the old tastes deserve to live on, made honestly.

Founded by Preeti Agrawal, our house selects every ingredient with care — real pudina, true anardana, fragrant saunf, and pure hing — so that each product carries the warmth of a family recipe, not a factory formula.

Every pack that leaves our house is FSSAI registered, GST compliant, and sealed with the promise our logo carries in gold.
